| About Us

The Artistic Building Company

AD Constructions is a construction company based in  Ambattur, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded by Mr. Nethaji Kathirvel, a visionary entrepreneur who also established Aesthetic Designs, a leading architectural and interior design firm in the region. We have been in the business since 2007 and have completed many successful projects for our clients.

Our Mission is to provide high-quality construction services that meet the needs and expectations of our clients.
Our Vision is to become a leading construction company in the region, known for our excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our core values are integrity, professionalism, teamwork, safety, and sustainability. We adhere to the highest ethical standards and deliver on our promises. We respect our clients, partners, employees, and the environment. We work together to achieve our goals and overcome challenges. We ensure the safety of our workers and the public at all times. We use eco-friendly materials and practices to minimize our environmental impact.

You should choose AD Constructions because we have the experience, expertise, and equipment to handle any type of construction project. Some of the reasons why we are the best choice for your construction needs are:
  • We have CMDA Registered Engineers for Chennai projects
  • We do Turnkey Residential Projects for hassle-free living
  • We offer Project Management Services for smooth execution
