AD Constructions is a construction company based in Ambattur, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded by Mr. Nethaji Kathirvel, a visionary entrepreneur who also established Aesthetic Designs, a leading architectural and interior design firm in the region. We have been in the business since 2007 and have completed many successful projects for our clients.

Our Mission is to provide high-quality construction services that meet the needs and expectations of our clients.
Our Vision is to become a leading construction company in the region, known for our excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our core values are integrity, professionalism, teamwork, and sustainability. We adhere to the highest ethical standards and deliver on our promises. We respect our clients, partners, employees, and the environment. We work together to achieve our goals and overcome challenges. We use eco-friendly materials and practices to minimize our environmental impact.

Because we are the Best in the Business. We are Transparent, Quality-Oriented, Efficient, and Safe. We show you the Proof of our Work, We Deliver on our Promises, We Manage our Projects Well, and We Ensure the Safety of our workers and the public at all times. We also have the Experience, Expertise, and Equipment to handle any type of construction project.
